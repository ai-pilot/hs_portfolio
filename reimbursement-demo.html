AI Fraud Detection Pipeline — 100% Automated Audit
📎
Ingest
SAP / Fusion / Any
Receipts ingested from expense portals (Concur, SAP, Oracle Fusion), mobile apps, email, and scanned documents. EXIF metadata (GPS, timestamp, device ID) captured. Supports 40+ languages across 97 countries.
➔
📄
OCR + LLM Parse
Document AI
Document Intelligence extracts structured fields. LLM contextually understands what's on the receipt — not just text, but meaning: identifying hidden charges, miscategorized items, and non-standard line items. Handles thermal, handwritten, and low-quality scans.
➔
🔎
Digital Forensics
AI-Fake Detection
Multi-layer authenticity: (1) Computer vision detects GAN/diffusion artifacts, pixel uniformity, and synthetic noise patterns. (2) EXIF metadata validation — missing GPS/device data flags fake uploads. (3) Digital fingerprinting matches against known AI receipt templates. (4) Merchant registry cross-reference. AI-fake receipts rose from 0% to 14% of fraud in 2025.
➔
📜
Policy Engine
Rules + NLP
Validates against live company policy: per-diem caps, restricted categories (alcohol, tobacco, entertainment, spa), weekend/holiday claims, receipt age limits, split-transaction detection, location vs. itinerary matching, and Benford's Law distribution checks on amounts.
➔
👥
Behavioral AI
Baseline Deviation
Per-employee behavioral model tracks: spending patterns vs. peer group, frequency anomalies, merchant preferences, timing patterns, and geolocation consistency. Flags deviations from established baselines — catching gradual escalation patterns human auditors miss.
➔
🔍
Duplicate + Collusion
Graph Analysis
Image hashing + fuzzy matching + graph-based network analysis: detects same receipt across employees (collusion rings), split transactions to bypass limits, resubmitted rejected claims, and cross-report duplicates. Graph methods reveal hidden relationships between claimants and merchants.
➔
📊
Risk Scoring
Ensemble Model
Stacking ensemble (Isolation Forest + XGBoost + Neural Network) scores 0-100. Features: amount vs. peer average, merchant price validation against known databases, Benford's Law conformity, historical flag rate, behavioral deviation score, and 40+ engineered signals.
➔
🎯
Auto-Triage
99% No-Touch
Intelligent routing: auto-approve clean claims (99% processed without human touch), auto-reject confirmed fraud, escalate policy violations to managers, and queue suspicious patterns for audit. Full AI reasoning audit trail for every decision. Finance dashboard with drill-down analytics.
